# Product Overview

KinetixLab is a biomechanics video analysis application designed for Singapore PE education. It analyzes student movement videos to assess Fundamental Movement Skills (FMS) against the MOE PE Syllabus standards.

## Core Functionality

- Upload and scrub through exercise videos (squats, throws, kicks, etc.)
- Real-time pose detection using MediaPipe for joint tracking
- AI-powered biomechanical analysis via Google Gemini
- Step-by-step movement breakdown with correctness feedback
- Joint angle measurements compared against ideal ranges
- Safety ratings and actionable corrections
- Analysis history with thumbnail previews

## Target Users

PE teachers and students in Singapore schools evaluating movement technique against official FMS checklists and proficiency rubrics.
